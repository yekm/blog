## Beet Gaye Din

Beet gaye din bhajan binaa re  
Beet gaye din bhajan binaa re

Baal avasthaa khel gavaayi  
Jab joban tab maan ghanaa re  
Beet gaye din bhajan binaa re

Laahe kaaran mool gavaayo  
Ajahoon na gayi man ki trishnaa re  
Beet gaye din bhajan binaa re

Kahat Kabir suno bhai saadho  
Paar utar gaye sant janaa re  
Beet gaye din bhajan binaa re
