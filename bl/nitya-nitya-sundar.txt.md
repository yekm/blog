## Nitya Nitya Sundar


Nitya nitya sundar mukhaar vindam  
Naache re nandalaalaa... nandalaalaa  
Bhakto ke rakhawalaa... nandalaalaa  
Gopiyon ka gopaalaa... nandalaalaa  
Naache re nandalaalaa.... nandalaalaa  
Meera ka matwalaa.... nandalaalaa  
Nitya nitya sundar mukhaar vindam  
Naache re nandalaalaa... nandalaalaa
