Shaktisahita Shiva Natana Manohara

Bhola Shankara bhola, Hara bhola Shankara bhola  
Nandi vaahana naga bhooshana  
Shaktisahita Shiva Natana Manohara

Bhola Shankara bhola, Hara bhola Shankara bhola  
Surya Chandra Pralaya trinetra dhara  
Shaktisahita Shiva Natana Manohara  
Bhola Shankara bhola, Hara bhola Shankara bhola
